High School

Starting my journey at MAN 1 Jember as an accelerated program student was both thrilling and overwhelming. From day one, I could feel the energy and high expectations that came with being part of this special program. The orientation introduced us to the intense yet exciting curriculum, designed to challenge us and maximize our potential.

Although the workload was heavier than I was used to, I felt proud to be part of a program that values hard work and excellence. I’m excited to embrace this challenge and grow both academically and personally during my time at MAN 1 Jember.
